Description

An efficient embroidery machine, the Memory Craft 450 E embroidery machine offers a speed of 860 spm (stitches per minute) and is enabled to embroider designs of up to 200 X 280 mm making it great for boutiques. It has two hoops – RE28b: 8” x 11” and SQ20b: 8” x 8”, which are adjustable even after embroidery has begun. Its extra wide table supports large projects and its free Digitizer Jr V5 software allows editing of existing designs and creation of customised designs
